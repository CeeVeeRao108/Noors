Where it began

A promise from Bhargav,
a recipe from Noorain.

Noor's began as a simple frustration: biryani had stopped tasting like biryani. Colours came from a bottle, meat sat frozen for weeks, and "fresh" was just a word on a menu. Bhargav set out to build something different — a kitchen that would show people what authentic biryani actually tastes like.

Every handi is prepared using recipes and technique from Noorain, cooked in small batches with fresh mutton and chicken sourced on the day of cooking. No artificial colour goes into the rice — the amber you see is from real saffron and slow-caramelised onions, exactly as it should be.

We only cook on demand. There's no stockpile, no reheating, no shortcuts — just authentic Bangalore-style biryani, entirely halal, made fresh and delivered the way it left the handi.
